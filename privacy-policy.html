Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2026

Wavadio ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and stored when you use the Wavadio mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Wavadio does not require user accounts and does not ask users to provide personal information such as names or email addresses.

We may collect limited, non-personal information to improve app performance and stability. This may include:

  • Crash reports and device information via Crashlytics
  • App interaction and usage data stored locally on your device
  • Approximate location (used only for selecting radio stations)

No personal user information is collected or shared.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by Wavadio is used to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Analyze crashes to fix issues

Wavadio does not use collected information for advertising or track you across other apps or websites.

3. Data Handling and Storage

  • Local data: Favorites, recent history, and custom stations are stored locally on your device.
  • Backup / Restore: You can manually backup and restore your data on this device.
  • Cloud sync (future feature): Optional cloud sync may allow data to be stored in iCloud for access across devices.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Crashlytics (Firebase): Used to collect crash reports and device information to improve app stability. Data is anonymous and does not include personal information.
  • Radio-Browser API: Used to fetch radio station information. No personal user data is sent to this service.

5. Data Sharing

  • Wavadio does not sell personal information.
  • Information may be shared only with service providers that support app functionality, crash reporting, or analytics, or when required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

Wavadio is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You may control app-related data through your device settings, including:

  • Removing the app to stop further local data collection
  • Managing backups stored locally on your device
  • In the future, deleting synced data if cloud backup is used

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any material changes will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
